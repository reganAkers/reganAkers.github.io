Regan Akers

Who am I?

My name is Regan, I have just recently graduated from Staffordshire University with a degree in Computer Games Development, looking to gain some experience in the industry.

What Am I Currently Working On?

I am currently working on my recent mobile game 1ManArmy, preparing it for my Grad Ex showcase. I am also delving deeper into design patterns and learning the basics of shaders at the moment.

About Me

Firewatch, World of Warcraft and Rust are my three biggest inspirations for wanting to make video games. I like to keep active by playing basketball, going to the gym and bouldering in my free time.
